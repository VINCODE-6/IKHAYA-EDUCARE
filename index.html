About Us

A caring start for every child

Ikhaya Educare Centre is a trusted childcare agency in the heart of Browns Farm, dedicated to early childhood development through attentive care, joyful learning, and a welcoming community for families. Founded in 2008, the centre was established Hilda Masiza who wanted to create a safe, stable, and inspiring place for local children to grow while supporting working families.

Our approach was built on the belief that every child deserves a strong early foundation. We combine structure, play, nourishment, and personal care so children can explore confidently, develop socially, and feel seen in a space that feels like home.
